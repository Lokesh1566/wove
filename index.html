●  Our story

You already own enough to look incredible.

Wove is building the stylist that lives in your closet, not the store. A little AI and a little craft, pointed at the clothes you forgot you had.

01

The mission

It is the paradox almost everyone lives with. You stand in front of a full closet and feel like there is nothing to wear. Most people regularly reach for only a fraction of what they own, while the industry keeps producing faster than the planet can carry. The shortage was never clothes. It is the connection to the ones already hanging there.

Wove exists to close that gap. By turning your wardrobe into something your phone can see and understand, we help you rediscover forgotten pieces, build outfits you would not have thought of, and make any new purchase a deliberate one. The goal is a world where people dress better by knowing what they have, not by buying more.

02

How it started

Wove began with a familiar frustration: too many mornings lost in front of a packed wardrobe, and a hunch that the tools to fix it already existed and just needed to be assembled well. It is built by an engineer who has spent years shipping machine learning into real products, and it started as an experiment in whether software could make getting dressed easier, and maybe even a small pleasure.
